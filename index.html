    About

    Did you ever wonder what time it is in places you are not at? Do you prefer your clocks to be analog and your interfaces minimal? Do you like having multiple of them on standby for that cosmopolitan feel? Then this is for you.

    World:Time is a practice project entirely developed in vanilla js without the use of any frameworks or external libraries.
